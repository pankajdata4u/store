Our technology

Our technology couples the innovation of an open source development model with the stability of a true enterprise-class platform. It has led to rapid development and adoption of Futureopsis Enterprise applications—from the Fortune 500 to enterprises of all sizes. We believe that an open platform can not only outpace innovation of proprietary content management platforms—but also deliver greater flexibility in today’s environment where highly adaptable, globally distributed organizations are the norm. To that end, Futureopsis technology is now available as a true, multi-tenant cloud service with native mobile applications for iOS and Android. With an open, standards-based API and open source mobile SDKs, Futureopsis continues to leverage the power of open standards and open source to deliver value for customers - even in the cloud.
